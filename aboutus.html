About

PennyJuice is a family-owned business that was founded by the Jones family. It all started when Mrs. Jones wanted to provide a healthy and delicious beverage option for her children. She experimented with different fruits and flavors until she found the perfect blend. Her children loved it so much that they started sharing it with their friends. Soon, the demand for the juice grew and the family decided to turn it into a business. The Jones family takes pride in sourcing the freshest and highest quality ingredients for their juices. They are committed to providing a healthy and tasty option for families everywhere. Today, PennyJuice has expanded its reach and offers a variety of flavors to choose from. Despite their growth, the family remains dedicated to their core values and mission of providing delicious and healthy beverages.
12 of our tropical flavors are inspired by the islands and beaches of the Caribbean, Hawaii, and other tropical paradises. These flavors are perfect for those looking to escape the everyday and indulge in something exotic and refreshing. Some of our top tropical flavors include pineapple, mango, coconut, passionfruit, and guava. Each flavor is carefully crafted to capture the essence of the fruit, with just the right balance of sweetness and tartness. Whether enjoyed on their own or mixed into a delicious cocktail, our tropical flavors are sure to transport you to a sun-soaked beach with every sip.
Tropical beverages offer a unique flavor that you might not get in your home country. Whether you're looking for an exotic cocktail or a refreshing fruity drink, you can find it in the wide selection of tropical beverages that are now available to ship worldwide. From Hand-crafted Brazilian Caipirinhas to flavorful Brazilian Tapiocas, these drinks are sure to offer an exciting twist to your typical drinks. Caribbean drinks like the Jamaican overproof rum offerings are also available to be shipped worldwide and provide a unique flavor that is sure to surprise your palate. Not to mention the Caribbean blended drinks with just the right flavor that won't be forgotten. For those looking for something a little sweeter, the Brazilian Caipifrutas are the perfect drinks that provide a smooth yet delicious combination of fruits and syrups that will tantalize your taste buds and quench your thirst. No matter what taste you’re looking for, tropical beverages are now only a click away and can be shipped right to your door!

Apple Juice
Posted October 29 2023 by billy bob joe

Apple juice is a beloved beverage that often gets overlooked in favor of fancier drinks. However, there are five excellent reasons why apple juice should get more recognition. Firstly, it can offer a refreshing alternative when water starts to become bland. Secondly, it packs a nutrition punch, with high levels of antioxidants such as vitamin C, quercetin and chlorogenic acid. Thirdly, it’s an easily accessible source of polyphenols, which can improve blood pressure, reduce cholesterol levels and aid digestion. Fourthly, apple juice contains energy-boosting nutrients like fructose, which make it an ideal wake-up beverage or pre-workout drink. Lastly, apple juice is naturally delicious, with its crisp and sweet flavor. Whether used as a drink, added to smoothies or used as an ingredient in recipes, there’s no denying the versatility of apple juice. Therefore, it’s high time that apple juice get its deserved recognition and respect.
